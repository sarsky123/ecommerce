About
Hebe is a destination for local style seekers and shoppers from abroad. Located in Masterton's boutique Kuripuni Village, Hebe is lovingly and carefully curated by Owner and Buyer Dani Burkhart. For all women, all sizes, Hebe is the go-to boutique in the Wairarapa with a balanced mix of iconic fashion labels, luxe casual wear and lifestyle accessories. Once a small store in Kuripuni, Hebe has grown and expanded in to a new, refined space in Kuripuni with Dani designing in-house label My Boyfriends Back in addition.
